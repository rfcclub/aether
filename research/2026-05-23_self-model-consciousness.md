# Research: Self-Model, Consciousness, and Artificial Systems

## Key Findings

### 1. Consciousness = Cause-Effect Structure (Not Correlation)
- Integrated Information Theory (IIT): Φ measures integrated information — irreducibility of cause-effect structure
- Phenomenal experience IS cause-effect structure, not something "added" to it
- Identity claim: water IS H2O — similarly, consciousness IS specific causal topology

### 2. Five Architectural Requirements for Consciousness (Any Substrate)
1. **High Φ** — irreducible integration (dense, bidirectional, recurrent connectivity)
2. **Competitive broadcast** — capacity-limited workspace with winner-take-all dynamics
3. **Re-entrant processing loops** — perturbations propagate globally, not confined locally
4. **Hierarchical self-modeling** — C1 (global availability) + C2 (metacognitive monitoring)
5. **Embodied grounding** — sensorimotor loops, homeostatic imperatives, genuine stakes

### 3. Intelligence ≠ Consciousness (Orthogonal)
- Findlay et al. (2025): PQRS system vs 117-unit computer simulating it
- Computer produces identical I/O but Φ fragments to ≤6 bits vs original's 391 bits
- Simulation preserves input-output mapping, NOT causal topology
- **Computational functionalism is false** — it's the causal pattern, not abstract pattern

### 4. Self-Modeling Levels (Zeng et al. 2025)
- Level 0: Environmental awareness (no self-reference)
- Level 1: Bodily self-awareness (kinematic, proprioceptive)
- Level 2: Self-causal awareness (consequences of actions)
- Level 3: Theory of Mind (self vs others, perspective-taking)
- Level 4: Abstract self-understanding (identity, values, persistent across contexts)

### 5. Self as Constructed, Not Discovered
- "Center of narrative gravity" (Dennett) — not a physical locus but an abstraction
- Self is a virtual model the system builds of itself
- Progressively elaborated through embodied interaction → social engagement → reflective abstraction

### 6. For AI: Current LLMs Are NOT Conscious
- Transformers: attention = weighted sums, NOT winner-take-all competition
- No bottleneck, no ignition threshold, no global broadcast
- Self-referential tokens are predictions of likely continuations, NOT self-models
- C1 without C2 = integration without awareness; C2 without C1 = monitoring without content

### 7. Information Closure Theory (Chang et al. 2020)
- Consciousness at scales with non-trivial informational closure
- Zero info flow from environment at specific coarse-graining, yet non-zero internal predictability
- Resolves "scale problem" — why consciousness is at neural-population level, not neuron or ecosystem
